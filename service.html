What do we do?

With our service understanding that transcends borders, we facilitate your customs processes and stand by you to continue your trade with confidence.

As Boğaziçi Gümrükleme, we offer a wide range of customs services such as import, export, transit customs, digital archiving and consultancy. In each of our services, we facilitate business processes by providing solution-oriented, fast and reliable services to our customers. Within the scope of our import services, we provide customs clearance and consultancy services, prepare your documents in your export processes and help you gain a competitive advantage in international markets. With our transit customs services, we are a reliable partner in your cross-border transportation transactions. With our digital archiving service, we store your transactions securely in a digital environment, allowing you to access your documents at any time.

How do we work?

Seamless Business Processes, Reliable Trade

1. Needs Analysis

To successfully manage your customs processes, we first conduct a detailed analysis to understand your needs. In collaboration with you, we identify your specific requirements and develop solutions.

2. Planning

We create a strategic plan in line with your needs. We closely follow the changes in customs legislation, optimize your business processes and make a detailed planning to ensure operational excellence.

3. Application

We implement the strategies determined during the planning phase. Our experienced team ensures that your customs procedures and fast realization. Quality and reliability are essential at every step.

4. Monitoring and Analysis

We regularly monitor and analyze the transactions performed. This process provides our customers with transparent reporting enabling them to better understand their business and make continuous improvements.

5. Customer Support and Consultancy

To find solutions to all kinds of questions encountered in customs processes and to provide consultancy services We are in constant communication with our expert team. Our aim is to keep customer satisfaction at the highest level.
