Temporary Import Guide

What does temporary import mean?

Temporary import is a customs regime that allows goods imported for a specific period to be re-exported in the same condition after the expiration of that period.

Can goods imported through temporary import undergo processing or work in Turkey?

Yes, goods imported through temporary import can undergo processing or work under certain conditions. However, this is subject to customs regulations and regime conditions.

Is there a time limit in the Temporary Import Regime?

Yes, the Temporary Import Regime has a specific time limit. The imported goods must be re-exported within a certain period.

How are full and partial exemptions applied in temporary import procedures?

Full or partial exemptions in temporary import procedures may vary depending on customs regulations and regime conditions. Generally, goods meeting specific criteria may qualify for exemptions.

Which goods are not eligible for temporary import regime?

Some goods, especially those of high value or subject to specific restrictions, may not be eligible for the temporary import regime.

Can you permanently import goods imported temporarily?

Yes, goods imported temporarily can be permanently imported under certain conditions. However, this is subject to customs regulations and regime conditions.

Re-exportation of goods imported temporarily

Goods imported temporarily must be re-exported in the designated period. Otherwise, appropriate procedures must be followed in accordance with customs regulations.

What happens in case of violation of temporary import regime rules?

In case of a violation of temporary import regime rules, sanctions determined by the customs administration may be applied. These sanctions may vary depending on the nature of the violation.

What documents are used in addition to the customs declaration in temporary import procedures?

In temporary import procedures, in addition to the customs declaration, documents such as invoices, transport documents, insurance policies are commonly used.
