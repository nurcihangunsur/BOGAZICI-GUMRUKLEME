Customs Declaration Guide

What is a customs declaration?

A customs declaration is an official document presented to the competent authorities for a commodity to undergo customs procedures.

What does the term "Customs Approved Procedure or Use" mean?

This term signifies that a commodity subject to customs procedures has undergone a specific customs procedure.

What is a customs regime?

A customs regime determines whether a commodity is subject to customs procedures and under what conditions it will be processed.

What is a Customs Declaration?

A customs declaration is an official document submitted to authorities to subject a commodity to customs procedures. It contains information such as the description, quantity, value, and other essential details of the goods.

What information is included in a customs declaration?

A customs declaration includes information such as the description, quantity, value, country of origin, customs duties, and other significant details of the goods.

What documents are attached to a customs declaration?

Typically, documents such as invoices, transport documents, and certificates of origin are attached to a customs declaration.

What documents are used instead of a customs declaration?

Documents with international validity, such as ATA Carnet and TIR Carnet, can be used instead of a customs declaration.

Why is it important to record accurate information on the customs declaration?

Recording accurate information on the customs declaration ensures smooth and fast customs procedures, and it is also a legal requirement.

Can changes be made to the information registered on the customs declaration?

Yes, changes can be made to the information registered on the customs declaration, but it is important to inform the competent authorities of these changes.

Can you cancel your customs declaration?

Customs declarations are generally cancellable at the beginning of the process, but it might be challenging to cancel once the process has started.

Is it possible to make a customs declaration in a simpler way?

Yes, in some cases, it is possible to make a customs declaration through a simplified process. For example, there may be a simplified declaration process among customs union countries.

What is an Oral Declaration?

An oral declaration is when the owner of the goods verbally presents the description and other information about the goods to the customs official.

What is ETGB?

ETGB (Electronic Commerce Customs Declaration) is a system that allows the electronic submission of customs declarations.

Which transactions are carried out under the ETGB application?

The ETGB application is generally used for customs procedures of low-value, small-volume goods that meet specific criteria.
