Taxation Guide

How are customs duties calculated?

Customs duties are calculated based on the customs value of the imported goods, import regime, and other factors.

Which taxes are levied on import transactions?

Import transactions generally incur taxes such as customs duties, VAT (Value Added Tax), and excise duty.

What is the taxation procedure of the customs administration for imports?

The customs administration carries out taxation procedures for imports in accordance with the customs tariff schedule and other regulations.

Under what circumstances can penalties be imposed by the customs administration?

The customs administration may impose penalties on individuals who fail to comply with customs rules or provide incomplete declarations.

Can you contest customs duties and fines notified for payment?

Yes, you have the right to contest customs duties and fines that you are required to pay. The objection should be submitted in writing to the customs administration.

What happens if you fail to pay customs duties on time?

If you fail to pay customs duties on time, late payment interest may apply, and your customs procedures may be suspended.

Can you reclaim paid customs duties and fines?

You may have the chance to reclaim or request a reduction in paid customs duties and fines under certain circumstances.

Under what conditions do accrued but unpaid customs duties and fines disappear?

Accrued but unpaid customs duties and fines may be extinguished under certain conditions, such as reaching a settlement.

Under what circumstances is a refund or removal process conducted?

The refund or removal process related to customs duties or fines can be initiated upon request or at the initiative of the customs administration under specific circumstances.

When should refund or removal procedures be carried out?

Refund or removal procedures are generally requested within a certain period after the accrual of customs duties or fines.

Which authority is responsible for refund or removal procedures?

Refund or removal procedures are typically conducted by the customs administration or the relevant authority.

How is an application for refund or removal made?

An application for refund or removal is submitted in writing to the customs administration and must be supported by the necessary documents.

What should be done if it is realized that customs duties were undercharged or not charged as required due to your customs procedures?

If it is realized that customs duties were undercharged or not charged as required due to your customs procedures, the customs administration may undertake correction measures.

Can reductions be applied to customs duties and fines arising from your customs procedures?

Reductions can be requested in certain situations for customs duties and fines arising from your customs procedures.

Which taxes and fines fall within the scope of settlement?

Under certain circumstances, customs duties and fines may fall within the scope of settlement, and an agreement can be reached between the parties.

When and how can a settlement be requested?

A settlement request is typically made in writing to the customs administration within a certain period after the accrual of customs duties or fines.

What conditions are necessary to request a settlement?

To request a settlement, an application can be made for customs duties or fines above a certain amount. Additionally, conditions such as correcting errors or special circumstances may be required.

How is a settlement request evaluated?

A settlement request is examined by the customs administration, and if deemed appropriate based on the claims of the applicant, a settlement may be reached.

What is the outcome of the evaluation of a settlement request?

The evaluation of a settlement request results in the acceptance of the settlement by the customs administration and specifies under what conditions the settlement is accepted.
