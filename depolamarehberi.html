Storage / Warehouse Guide

What does temporary storage mean?

Temporary storage is the retention of goods in a customs warehouse for a specific period while awaiting the completion of customs procedures.

What is a customs warehouse?

A customs warehouse is a private storage area under customs control where goods are stored without payment of customs duties.

Who can open/operate a customs warehouse?

The authority to open and operate a customs warehouse generally belongs to organizations or companies designated by the customs administration.

What goods can be placed in a customs warehouse?

Goods subject to customs control during import or export procedures are typically placed in a customs warehouse.

How long can you keep your goods in the warehouse?

The duration of keeping goods in the warehouse may vary depending on customs regulations and the policies of the warehouse operator.

How many types of customs warehouses are there?

Customs warehouses can have different types, such as general warehouse, special warehouse, free warehouse, and others.

Who is responsible for goods placed in the warehouse/What are the responsibilities of warehouse operators and users?

Warehouse operators are responsible for the security and preservation of goods belonging to warehouse users. Users are obligated to fulfill customs obligations related to the goods stored in the warehouse.

How do you bring your goods into the warehouse?

To bring goods into the warehouse, you must obtain permission from the customs administration, complete the necessary documents, and coordinate with the warehouse operator.

How are taxes on goods placed in the warehouse handled?

Taxes on goods placed in the warehouse are paid when the goods are removed from the warehouse. The amount of tax is determined according to customs regulations.

What is the scope and permit procedure for handling goods placed in the warehouse?

Handling operations for goods placed in the warehouse are carried out within the rules set by the customs administration. Handling permits must be obtained from customs authorities.

How do you remove your goods from the warehouse?

To remove goods from the warehouse, you must obtain permission from the customs administration, complete the necessary documents, and coordinate with the warehouse operator.

What is shared storage?

Shared storage is a storage area open for joint use by multiple companies or individuals. It often provides logistical and cost advantages.
